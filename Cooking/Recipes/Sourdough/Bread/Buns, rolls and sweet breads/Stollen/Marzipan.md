## Directions:

1. Toast the nuts if they are raw (although it's traditional to use blanched raw). Off of the heat. 
2. Whip the egg whites until they are slightly foamy and uniform.
3. If using granulated sugar, process it first (in two batches if using the smaller food processor: half nuts amount, half sugar). Blend; pulsing (if using hot nuts; otherwise - just blend thoroughly). 
4. Add salt and rum and process again. Transfer to a bowl and add egg whites, gather into a dough.
5. Shape into a log, wrap tightly in plastic and store in the fridge up to two weeks.
