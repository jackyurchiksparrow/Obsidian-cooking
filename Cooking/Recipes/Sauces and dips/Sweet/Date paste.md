Ingredients:
1.2 kg dates
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
water (20-40ml)
1-1.5 tbsp. lemon juice
spirit-based vanilla extract

1. Remove the pits and the upper tough part if it's present. If the dates are tough, pit them after the next step.
2. Add baking soda and salt to a bowl with dates. Fill with boiling water for 30min. Strain.
3. Turn the soaked dates into paste. While blending, add water, lemon juice and vanilla extract. Enjoy. Store in the fridge for 2-3+ weeks.
