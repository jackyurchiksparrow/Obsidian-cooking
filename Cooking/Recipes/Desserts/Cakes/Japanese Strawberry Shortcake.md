> IMPORTANT
> - Do not assemble 28cm cakes anymore. You won't be able to cover them in the fridge with the biggest container. Do 26cm.
> - Since this recipe requires heavy cream, not whipping, it expects more fat and therefore stability from it. Add gelatin to the outer layer as well if you want to stabilize it, or better use mascarpone since immediate clumping is a concern when applying the outer layer. Might not apply the outer layer at all, making it Fraisier-style.
