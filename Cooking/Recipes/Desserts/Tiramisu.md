Tiramisu  
  
1kg Mascarpone  
900 g whipping cream  
226 g egg yolk (11-12 yolks)  
213 g sugar  
93 g water  
Strong coffee  

For orange version, incorporate into the final cream layer:
- **220g** Fresh orange juice (strained)
- **40g** Sugar
- **14g** Cornstarch
- **Zest of 2 oranges**

> [!Warning]
> Never whip mascarpone cheese with cream, the fat content is so high it easily overwhips and turns into pure fat


① Lightly beat the mascarpone cheese. Put into the fridge. 
② Whip the heavy cream until almost stiff peaks (not too soft). Mix the mascarpone cheese in and beat on lowest until just incorporated while you work on the pâté à bombe.
If making the orange flavor base for the very top layer, make it now and let it cool in the fridge.
③ Pâté à bombe: Add egg yolk + water + sugar in a saucepan and raise it to 85℃ using a hot water bath. I recommend straining it. Whip it until you get a light yellow color.  
④ Add the pâté à bombe to the mascarpone & heavy cream that we’ve whipped earlier. Mix everything well and whip it again until you get a stiffer consistency.  
⑤ Pour it over the coffee syrup soaked lady fingers.  
⑥ Let it chill/set in the fridge for at least 3~4hrs.  
⑦ Dust it with cocoa powder and serve!
