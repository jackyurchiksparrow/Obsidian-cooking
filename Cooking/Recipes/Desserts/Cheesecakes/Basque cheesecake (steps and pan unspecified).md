## Directions:

1. Ideally, all ingredients must be at room temperature to properly bake.
2. Prepare the pan by lining the bottom of a springform pan with parchment and then another layer of parchment on top to cover all the sides and the bottom one more time (crumple and unfold the parchment to better fit it in).
3. Blend *cottage cheese* and *cream* together with *sugar* and *salt*.
	> (break the blocks of cheese first not to overheat the blender).
4. In a separate bowl, mix the *eggs* with *cornstarch*. Add them to the batter and mix again.
	> do not blend the eggs, just mix. we don't want to incorporate air not to mess up with the texture (we don't want it spongy)
5. Add *vanilla*.
6. STRAIN into the springform for smoothness.
7. Bake at max temperature NO FAN for ~15-20 min (for 16cm) until the top level is deep bronze (don't have to burn it) and a wobbly center and edges. Remove it from the oven immediately. Cool and refrigerate overnight.
8. Glaze when cool for shine if desired.
